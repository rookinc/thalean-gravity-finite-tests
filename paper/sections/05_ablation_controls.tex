\section{Ablation and controls}

A five-edge carrier-sign toggle set was found that neutralizes all odd carrier triangles:
\[
    [0,1],\ [0,4],\ [1,2],\ [2,3],\ [5,10].
\]

In the original graph, the return-pair profile is
\[
    \{-3:22,\,-1:272,\,0:606\},
\]
with 294 compressed pairs and mean delta
\[
    -0.37555555555555553.
\]

After neutralizing all odd carrier triangles, the profile becomes
\[
    \{-2:22,\,-1:8,\,0:870\},
\]
with 30 compressed pairs and mean delta
\[
    -0.057777777777777775.
\]

A same-size toggle control showed that this collapse is not generic five-edge sign damage. Among all five-edge toggle sets, only 243 of 142506 erase all odd carrier triangles, a fraction of approximately
\[
    0.0017051913603637742.
\]

A full metric sweep over all 243 zero-odd toggle sets showed that every zero-odd member is weaker than the original.
