\section{Boundaries}

This paper makes no claim that the mechanism is physical gravity.

It does not claim:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=2em]
\item that the odd carrier triangle is a physical graviton;
\item that the graph derives general relativity;
\item that the graph derives Newtonian gravity;
\item that the metric compression is a physical force;
\item that the result applies to all possible Thalean graphs.
\end{itemize}

The claim is bounded:

\begin{quote}
For this repaired finite carrier graph and this exact same-size toggle control regime, odd carrier triangle count organizes an exhaustive median return-compression gradient.
\end{quote}
