\section{Conclusion}

The repaired \(K900\) carrier graph exhibits a finite, measurable, and exhaustively controlled mechanism of return-path metric compression.

The main result is not merely that compression exists. It is that the compression is organized by odd carrier triangle count. The zero-odd class is weak. Positive odd classes are stronger. High odd-triangle classes saturate the compression profile.

The certified mechanism can be stated without physical language:

\begin{quote}
closed signed transport can make return shorter.
\end{quote}

In the internal terminology of Thalean graph theory, this is the first certificate-hardened finite gravity-like compression mechanism in Project 19. The theorem itself remains a bounded finite graph-theoretic result.
